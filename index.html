Swarajya Foundation (Sangli, Maharashtra)

Swarajya Foundation is a non-governmental organization (NGO) that stands as a beacon of hope and empowerment for women and children from marginalized communities. Its mission is to enhance the socio-economic status of these communities through comprehensive programs in education, healthcare, and skill development. By addressing the root causes of poverty and inequality, Swarajya Foundation aims to foster sustainable development and create lasting change.
